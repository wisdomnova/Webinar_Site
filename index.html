How to Use Webinar Slides

  1. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Open a Google Doc you want to convert.
  3. Click on the Webinar Slides icon in your Chrome toolbar.
  4. Follow the prompts to convert your document into a Google Slides presentation.
